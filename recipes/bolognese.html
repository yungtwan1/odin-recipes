Bolognese Sauce

What is Bolognese?

Bolognese is a kind of ragu (the Italian word for meat sauce), originally from Bologna, Italy.

Ingredients

  • Olive Olive
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Heavy Cream
  • Ground Beef & Ground Pork
  • Marinara Sauce
  • (Optional)Pasta Noodles Of Choice

Steps

  1. Saute the carrots, celery and onion
  2. Stir in pork and beef
  3. When the meat is lightly browned, pour in your marinara sauce(store bought or homemade)
  4. Simmer at least 30 while the mixture cooks down and thickens.
  5. In a separate pot, at some point while the sauce mixture simmers, boil some water for your choice of pasta
  6. Once the sauce mixture has reached the desired thickness, stir in the heavy cream.
  7. When the pasta is al dente, and the cream is fully incorporated into the sauce mixture, combine however you like into a serving dish
  8. Enjoy!
